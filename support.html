❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Go to the Pro tab and tap "Restore purchase." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
A product shows the wrong kosher status — what do I do?
Please contact us using the button below and include the country, product name, and the correct status. We review all reports and update listings promptly.
Why can't I find a specific product?
Not every product in every country is listed yet. We're constantly adding new products. If you'd like to suggest a product, please contact us.
How do I use Offline Mode?
With Pro, tap "Download for Offline" on any country's page. The full product list will be saved to your device and available without internet.
How often are products updated?
We regularly review and update our listings. However, kosher status can change at any time — always verify with the certifying agency before purchasing.
Is my personal data safe?
Yes. Kosher World does not collect any personal information. No account is required and we do not use tracking or advertising.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Kosher World → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
