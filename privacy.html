Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

📋 Overview

Kosher World ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

🔒 Data We Do Not Collect

Kosher World does not collect, store, or sell any personal information. Specifically:

• We do not require account creation or registration
• We do not collect your name, email, or location
• We do not use advertising or third-party tracking
• We do not share any data with third parties

📦 Product Data

Product listings are fetched securely from our servers (Cloudflare and Supabase) and temporarily cached on your device to improve performance. This data contains only kosher product information — no personal data is involved.

Pro users may save product lists to their device for offline use. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

💳 Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how purchase data is handled.

✡️ Our Commitment to Accuracy

We regularly review and update our kosher product listings to maintain the highest standard of accuracy. However, kosher status can change at any time. Always verify with the product's certifying agency before purchasing.

If you find an error in our listings, please contact us and we will correct it promptly.
